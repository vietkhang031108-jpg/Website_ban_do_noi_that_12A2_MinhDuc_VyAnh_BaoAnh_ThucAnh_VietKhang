    URBAN CARPET

    THE MODERN FOUNDATION

    A refined carpet designed to bring warmth and character into contemporary living spaces. Urban Carpet blends clean design with soft, durable fibers.

    Color

    Designed for modern living

    Every rhide piece is created with intention — balancing simplicity, comfort, and timeless aesthetics. Urban Carpet reflects our philosophy of quiet design that enhances everyday life.

    More than a carpet

    At rhide, we believe that design is not just about objects — it is about how those objects shape the way we live. Urban Carpet was created from a simple idea: to bring quiet comfort into everyday spaces without overwhelming them.

    Inspired by modern interiors and natural tones, each piece is designed to sit effortlessly within a room — grounding it, softening it, and giving it a sense of balance. It is not meant to stand out loudly, but to belong.

    Because true design is not seen first — it is felt.
